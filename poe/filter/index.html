Something... PoE Filter

Based on NeverSink Very Strict Blue. Softened (probably to the Strict or Semi-Strict level) and recoloured.

Idea was to have the filter with colours from NeverSink's 5.7 filter (Abyss-Bestiary league).

Low-tier currency colours (as I'm looking at them): Silver Coin Chromatic Orb pick it up pick it up if you want

Chaos/Regal recipe colours: body armour boots gloves helmets small 1h and 2h weapons large 2h weapons affect only T3 and lower items. Use large weapons as last resort.

Recoloured scarabs for some reason.

Recoloured 6-sockets to be as higher tier currency.

Highlighted maps with rare hideouts (Dig, Iceberg, Sunken City, Terrace, Tower). Highlight for maps with pre-Betrayal hideouts spawn can be enabled in FilterBlade separately.

Everything else is just as in NeverSink Very Strict filter
